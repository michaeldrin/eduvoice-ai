What is EduVoice?

EduVoice is an AI-powered document processing tool that transforms your learning materials into easy-to-digest summaries and natural-sounding audio. It helps you learn more efficiently by extracting the key information from documents and presenting it in formats that match your learning style.

Features

Upload Materials

Upload your own PDF and DOCX documents to process them with our advanced AI tools.

AI Summaries

Get instant AI-generated summaries that capture the essential information from your documents.

Natural Voice

Listen to your content in natural-sounding voice with interactive audio visualizer.

Customizable

Choose your preferred language and voice speed to match your learning preferences.
